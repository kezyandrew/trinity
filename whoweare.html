Who We Are

Get ready to dive into the heartbeat of worship at Nsambya Catholic Parish's Youth Encounter The Saviour Center (YES CENTER)!

Welcome to the heart of worship at Nsambya Catholic Parish's Youth Encounter The Saviour Center (YES CENTER). Trinity Worship Team has been a vibrant and integral part of our community for 13 years. Under the spiritual guidance of our Youth Chaplain, Father SSebunya, and led by a dynamic group of talented youths, we are committed to serving our congregation through the power of music and worship.

Our Mission And Values

At Trinity Worship Team, our mission is simple yet profound: to lead hearts and minds in worship, glorifying God through music, and nurturing a vibrant faith community.

  • 1
    Passion for Worship

    We are fueled by an unquenchable passion for worship. Our hearts beat in rhythm with the desire to create an atmosphere where people can encounter the living God. Worship isn't just what we do; it's who we are.

  • 2
    Unity in Diversity

    We celebrate diversity in all its forms. Our team is a vibrant tapestry of talents, backgrounds, and experiences. We embrace these differences as a source of strength, recognizing that unity in diversity is a reflection of God's love for all.

  • 2
    Spiritual Growth

    We are committed to personal and collective growth in our faith journeys. Through prayer, study, and reflection, we seek to deepen our relationship with God, constantly evolving as individuals and as a team.
